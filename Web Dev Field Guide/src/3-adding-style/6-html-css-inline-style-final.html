Teddy the Porcupine likes Corn on the Cob

"Teddy Bear" the Porcupine gets a cob of corn for a treat. Try and take it away from him and he complains.

Which one of the following statements is true?

A. Porcupines are excellent swimmers

B. Porcupines can throw their quills

C. Porcupines can climb trees

Answer: A and C

There are two types of Porcupines, New and Old World. Old World Porcupines that live in Southern Europe, Asia, and Africa are good swimmers, but poor climbers. New World Porcupines that live North America are good climbers and poor swimmers.
